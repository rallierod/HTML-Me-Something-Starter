“A DOOM BOX!”

You may be asking yourself — “What on earth is a doom box?” And that, my friend, is a valid question!

I present you with exhibit A:

A doom box is the box of things you don't know what to do with after you have used all of your bandwidth for cleaning or organizing for that day.

You throw all the extra random stuff into the box and then move it to the determined “doom” location. At a later time/date you remember the doom box and get to find the missing treasures you forgot about. So really it's a box that eventually sparks joy! Therefore it should make Marie Kando proud and should be considered a phenomenal organization strategy.🤣

This website is going to be like that. Just random treasures from my brain that spark joy!
