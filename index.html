Chaos Collector

I spent DAYS overanalyzing and thinking about what the contents of my website should include. I left the comfort and chaos of my house to walk down the street to my parent's house to sit on their couch and overanalyze the same things I had been overanalyzing for the days prior. I was staring into space when… 💥 BAM! It hit me! I could make my website, a “throw–it–in–box”! I know what you're thinking— ”What in the world is a 'throw–it–in–box'?” I present to you Exhibit A:

A place where you put all of the things when your bandwidth has run out for the day. It becomes a random assortment of treasures that you happily misplace for a while. Then one day you realize you need something that was in the box. You go to search through the box and that's when you find tons of missing treasures! You get distracted and forget what you were looking for in the first place! The spiral continues. This is my “throw-it-in-box”. In digital form...A random assortment of overanALLIEsis and magnificent treasures from my mind.
